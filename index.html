"There is a crack in everything; that's how the light gets in." —— excerpt from Anthem

krisymengni@gmail.com, krisymengni@163.com

I'm now working on inverse scattering problems of Alfvén waves governed by ideal incompressible magnetohydrodynamics (MHD) equations. My interests also extend to the behavior and stability of solutions to various equations arising in related wave phenomena and fluid dynamics. If time permits, I'm passionate about studying the well-posedness and qualitative properties of solutions to fully nonlinear elliptic equations over convex domains.
